From Agadir: Agadir city tour discovery

Explore the wonders of Agadir on a captivating city tour that will take you on a journey through its rich history and vibrant culture.

This full-day excursion from Agadir to Marrakech allows you to experience the diversity of Morocco's landscapes and cultural heritage.

The Agadir city tour includes visits to prominent landmarks such as the Kasbah of Agadir, which provides panoramic views of the city and its bay, as well as the opportunity to explore the city's bustling markets and the impressive Grand Mosque in Agadir.

Explore the gardens of the Koutoubia Mosque, walk through the famous square of Jemaa El Fna, try your bargaining skills in the local market, and get to see the walled Medina of Marrakech.

Notes to Consider :

• The local Market is closed on Mondays, so there is no Market stop on book tours on Mondays.

Itinerary :

➊ Pick up from your hotel or hostel at the selected time on the booking form.

➋ A short drive through the promenade of Agadir while driving through the Marina toward the old Kasbah.

➌ Panoramic view stop at Kasbah of Agadir, the most majestic site of the city, which is a fortress (was built in the 16th century and destroyed by the earthquake in 1960) overlooking the city, beach and the harbour, at a height of 236 meters.

➍ Drive through the back roads of the Agadir city and have a quick stop at the biggest mosque in Agadir.

➎ Optional stop at the cooperative of argan house for the first time visitors to learn about the argan oil process.

➏ Up to one hour and half of free time at the local market in Agadir, one of the largests markets in Morocco.

➐ Return back to the reception of your hotel after the free time in the Souk.
