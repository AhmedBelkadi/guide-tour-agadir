From Agadir: Croco parc tour

The Crocoparc tour from Agadir offers a captivating experience, allowing visitors to explore the park and observe over 300 Nile crocodiles, anacondas and other species, in a carefully designed botanical setting.

This tour provides an opportunity to learn about these fascinating creatures, their natural habitat, and conservation efforts.

Additionally, visitors can immerse themselves in the lush botanical displays and enjoy a unique and educational outing suitable for all ages.

Itinerary :

➊ Pick up from your hotel in Agadir or in Taghazout at the selected time on the booking form.

➋ A gentle drive of 30 minutes toward the highway of Agadir-Marrakech.

➌ Arriving to Crocoparc main entrance, and taking some photos at the main gate of the park.

➍ Receive your entrance ticket from your driver, and start your tour in the croco world.

➎ Enjoy up to 3 hours of free time in the Crocoparc.

➏ After the free time, we drive you back to your hotel.
