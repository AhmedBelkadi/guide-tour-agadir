From Agadir: Buggy tour

Buggy tour in Agadir offers an exciting and adventurous way to explore the region's diverse landscapes.

This tour provides participants with the opportunity to drive their own buggy or share it with a friend as a passenger, following a guide on a thrilling off-road adventure through shaded forests of Argan trees, sand dunes, and rural Berber areas.

This activity allows for an immersive and adrenaline-pumping experience, making it a popular choice for adventure enthusiasts seeking an exciting outdoor adventure in Agadir and its surrounding areas.

Notes to Consider :

• No driver's license is required for the buggy tour.
• The minimum age required to drive the Buggy is 16 years old.​
• When it comes to what to wear, we highly recommend choosing summer clothing along with suitable shoes.

Itinerary :

➊ Pick up from your hotel in Agadir at the selected time on the booking form.

➋ 30 minutes drive south of Agadir to the garage.

➌ Grab your safety equipments: Head dust-cover, helmet and goggles.

➍ A quick orientation for the first-time buggy riders.

➎ Start your buggy tour through the Argan trees and sand dunes and along the Atlantic ocean.

➏ Small break to rest, stretch your legs, before completing your buggy trip.

➐ After the buggy adventure, we will head back to the garage, and perform an air pressure cleaning to remove all the dust and sand, and then get you ready for the return journey to the hotel.
